# Privacy Policy - FS App (PitMonkey)

**Last updated:** September 2025  
**Effective date:** September 2025 

## Introduction

Welcome to PitMonkey FS. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email address:** Required for account creation and authentication
- **Full name:** For your user profile and team identification
- **Profile picture:** Optional, for profile customization
- **Password:** Securely encrypted and stored

### Team Information
- **Team name:** To identify and organize your racing team
- **Team logo:** Optional, for team branding
- **Team location:** Approximate location for map display and team discovery
- **Team description:** Optional details about your team

### Content You Create
- **Posts:** Text content, images, and media you share
- **Comments:** Your interactions with other users' content
- **Images:** Photos you upload for posts, profile, or team
- **Activity data:** Your app usage patterns for performance optimization

### Technical Information
- **Device information:** Device type, operating system, app version
- **Cache data:** Temporarily stored images and content for better performance

## How We Use Your Information

### Core Functionality
- **Authentication:** Verify your identity and secure your account
- **Team management:** Connect you with your team members and content
- **Content sharing:** Display your posts and enable team interactions
- **Map features:** Show team locations and help discover nearby teams

### App Performance
- **Caching:** Store images locally for faster loading
- **Memory management:** Optimize app performance on your device
- **Error handling:** Improve app stability and user experience

### Communication
- **Service updates:** Important app updates and security notifications
- **Support:** Respond to your questions and technical issues

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

### Within the App
- **Team members:** Can see your profile, posts, and team-related content
- **Public content:** Your team information may be visible to other users for team discovery
- **User interactions:** Comments and posts are visible to your team members

### Third-Party Services
- **Supabase:** Our secure database provider (data encrypted in transit and at rest)
- **No data selling:** We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information
- **No advertising:** We don't share data with advertising networks

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety.

## Data Security

### Protection Measures
- **Encryption:** All data transmitted using HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Secure storage:** Data stored in Supabase with enterprise-grade security
- **Access control:** Strict authentication and authorization protocols
- **Regular updates:** Security patches and updates applied promptly

### Local Storage
- **Image cache:** Images temporarily stored on your device for performance
- **Memory management:** Automatic cleanup of temporary data
- **No sensitive data:** Passwords and tokens never stored locally

- **Delete account:** Permanently remove your account and associated data

- **Content management:** Delete or edit your posts
- **Privacy settings:** Control visibility of your team and profile information

- **Contact us:** Email us for data export requests

- **Camera:** Access to take photos for posts and profile (optional)

## Data Retention

### Active Accounts
- We retain your data while your account is active and as needed to provide services

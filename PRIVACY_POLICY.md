# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: October 2025**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how PitMonkey FS collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable privacy laws.

## 2. Information We Collect

**Account Information:**
• Username, email, and profile picture
• Date of birth for age verification and legal compliance
• Team affiliation and profile details
• Country

**App Usage:**
• Team content and information you share
• User interactions and preferences
• User reports and safety preferences

**Automatically Collected:**
• Technical data necessary for app functionality
• Authentication and session data (managed by third-party database)
• Usage analytics for service improvement

**Competition Data:**
• Competition results are sourced from public information
• Provided for informational purposes only
• Official results must be verified with event organizers

## 3. How We Use Your Information

**We use your information to:**
• Provide app functionality (accounts, team profiles, content management)
• Enable approach between Formula Student community members
• Display competition information and team profiles
• Maintain app functionality, safety and compliance with our terms
• Ensure quality and appropriateness of community content using automated moderation tools when necessary
• Maintain app security and prevent abuse

**We do NOT:**
• Sell your data to third parties
• Track your behavior outside the app
• Share your data except as described in this policy

## 4. Data Sharing

**Third-Party Services:**
• Database hosting and user authentication
• Content moderation services for community safety when necessary
• These services have their own privacy policies and compliance

**External Content:**
• We may display logos and information from brands/companies, as well as links to external websites

**Legal Requirements:**
• We may disclose information when required by law
• To protect our rights and safety of users
• To investigate violations of our terms

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

**Data Retention:**
• Profile data: Removed when you delete your account
• Authentication data: Managed by third-party database according to their policies
• User content: May be retained as needed for app functionality and compliance
• Competition data: Retained as public historical information

**Security:**
• Data is encrypted by our service providers
• We rely on enterprise-grade security infrastructure
• No internet transmission is 100% secure
• Users are responsible for keeping account credentials secure

## 6. Your Rights

**You can:**
• Access and update your profile information
• Delete your account through app settings
• Control privacy settings and user interactions
• Report inappropriate content or behavior
• Have the right to data portability
• Contact us through the app. Contact information is displayed in the app

## 7. Age Requirements

• Our service is intended for users 16 years and older
• We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16
• If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information promptly

## 8. Changes to This Policy

• We may update this policy
• Continued use means acceptance of updates
• You can delete your account if you disagree with changes

**For privacy questions:**
• Use the in-app support feature
• Email: support@pitmonkeyfs.com
